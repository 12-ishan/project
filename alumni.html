About Us

The alumni are a lasting legacy and the strongest voice for any association. Finding new ways to build an engaged membership base is an integral task of the Association. one of the easiest ways to reconnect, give back to the Institute, and serve as a springboard for further involvement is through the CIE Alumni talk series. Alumni working in different fields share their expertise on a topic with the students through the Alumni talk series.
