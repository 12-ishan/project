About Us

The Departmental Society of Civil & Infrastructure Engineering (CIE) at IIT Jodhpur serves as a bridge between students, faculty, alumni, and industry, fostering a vibrant and collaborative departmental community. Functioning under the aegis of the Board of Departmental Societies and the Department of Civil and Infrastructure Engineering, the society is dedicated to cultivating meaningful interactions, shared learning, and a strong sense of belonging among all stakeholders.

CIE is committed to fostering technical growth, ethical practices, and industry-readiness through a range of initiatives such as seminars, workshops, technical sessions, and hands-on experiences. Through active student-faculty engagement, it also promotes leadership, collaboration, and sustainable thinking, in line with the society’s guiding motto: “Constructing a Better Future: Integrity in Design, Sustainability in Action, Cementing Community with Compassion.”

EDIFICIO is the flagship technical festival of the department, organized entirely by the student society. It offers budding civil engineers a national platform to explore and innovate through design challenges, workshops, lectures, panel discussions, and collaborative problem-solving. EDIFICIO continues to evolve as a space for experiential learning, interdisciplinary dialogue, and industry-academia synergy.

The society aspires to foster a nationally collaborative platform for the Civil Engineering discipline—bringing together academic institutions, industry stakeholders, and research partners to catalyze innovation, professional development, and cross-institute engagement. It envisions infrastructure as a true driver of human progress, bridging academia and industry, and empowering all who pursue excellence in this vital domain.
